About DJ03

In Sound We Trust

THE STORY

From Ibadan to England

The Journey Begins

Born and raised in Ibadan, Nigeria, DJ03 (born into the Iyiola family) is the youngest of three brothers. Growing up in a family of three, with two older brothers ahead of him, DJ03 learned early on the value of hard work, dedication, and carving his own path. His roots in Ibadan—a city known for its rich cultural heritage and vibrant music scene—shaped his musical foundation and instilled in him a deep appreciation for authentic African sounds.

Rising Through the Ranks

Now based in England, DJ03 has established himself as one of the most sought-after DJs in the UK music scene. With countless shows under his belt, he has proven time and again that he can command any stage, from intimate club settings to massive festival crowds. His ability to read the room and deliver exactly what the audience needs has made him a favorite among promoters and event organizers.

Behind the scenes, DJ03 is constantly at work—crafting sets, testing tracks, and perfecting his craft. This dedication to excellence is what sets him apart. Every mix is carefully curated, every transition is intentional, and every set tells a story.

Collaborations with Legends

DJ03's reputation extends far beyond the decks. He has had the honor of working alongside some of Africa's biggest music icons, including Buju, Tuface, Sheyi Vibez, and many other acclaimed artists. These collaborations have not only elevated his profile but have also enriched his understanding of music production, performance, and the business of entertainment.

Working with such established artists has taught DJ03 the importance of versatility, professionalism, and maintaining the highest standards in everything he does. Whether he's supporting a headline act or headlining his own show, DJ03 brings the same level of energy, skill, and passion to every performance.

The Art of the Mix

When DJ03 is on the mix, magic happens. His seamless blending of amapiano, afrobeat, and contemporary sounds creates an experience that is both nostalgic and forward-thinking. He understands that DJing is more than just playing tracks—it's about creating moments, building energy, and connecting with people through music.

Beyond the Decks

Off the stage, DJ03 maintains the same energy and authenticity that defines his performances. Whether he's unwinding after a show, networking with industry professionals, or spending time with family and friends, he carries himself with the same professionalism and passion that has made him successful.

As the last born of the Iyiola family, DJ03 has always been driven to prove himself and make his mark. That drive, combined with his natural talent and relentless work ethic, has positioned him as a force to be reckoned with in the music industry.

In Sound We Trust

DJ03's journey from Ibadan to England, from a family of three to working with music legends, is a testament to what's possible when talent meets dedication. With every show, every mix, and every collaboration, DJ03 continues to build his legacy—one beat at a time.

In Sound We Trust—because when DJ03 is on the decks, you know you're in for an experience you'll never forget.
